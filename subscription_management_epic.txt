Epic: Subscription Upgrade and Billing Management for Streaming App

Description:
As a subscribed user, I want to upgrade, downgrade, and manage my subscription plan with secure billing support so that I can choose the right plan for my needs without losing access unexpectedly.

Acceptance Criteria:

Users can access a subscription management page from their account settings.
Users can view their current subscription plan, renewal date, billing amount, and available upgrade or downgrade options.
Users can upgrade to a higher plan and receive immediate access to new benefits.
Users can downgrade to a lower plan, with the downgrade taking effect from the next billing cycle.
Users can update payment method details securely.
Users are shown prorated charges or credits before confirming a plan change.
Users receive confirmation after a successful subscription or billing change.
Users cannot switch to an invalid or unavailable plan.
Users see clear error messages for failed payments, invalid card details, or service outages.
Subscription and billing changes are logged for analytics and audit purposes.
Users receive an email notification after plan or billing updates.
System prevents duplicate billing actions when users retry after a timeout or refresh.
